# 🚲 BikeStore Data Analysis Project (SQL)

## Executive Summary
This project transforms raw transactional data from a multi-location bike retailer into actionable business insights. Using **MySQL**, I built a suite of queries to track growth, identify top talent, and measure customer loyalty.

## Technical Toolkit
- **Joins:** Multi-table joins across 5+ relational tables.
- **CTEs:** Structured complex logic for better readability and maintenance.
- **Window Functions:** Implemented `RANK()`, `ROW_NUMBER()`, and `LAG()` for time-series analysis.
- **Advanced Patterns:** Built Cohort Analysis and Retention models from scratch.

## Key Business Questions Answered
1. **Who are our Star Performers?** (Staff ranking by revenue per store).
2. **Is our revenue growing?** (Running totals and daily trends).
3. **Are customers coming back?** (Month-over-month retention and Cohort LTV).
